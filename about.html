About Our Academy

Our Community

La Crosse American Jiu Jitsu offers a fun, clean, and safe environment for both adults and kids to learn and train in jiu-jitsu. We pride ourselves on having some of the friendliest members in the community.

We encourage you to stop by and greet your new training partners. Discover firsthand how Jiu Jitsu can help you handle stress with ease, improve your breathing, and enhance your sleep quality.

Forge lifelong friendships on the mat as you progress from a white to a black belt. The journey is as much about personal growth as it is about martial arts proficiency.

No matter your age or lifestyle, La Crosse American Jiu Jitsu has a class for you. We offer specialized classes for Adults, Women, and Children, along with multiple open gym times throughout the week.

Proud Tom Deblass Affiliate

La Crosse American Jiu Jitsu is the only gym in the La Crosse/Winona area to be a Tom Deblass Affiliate. DeBlass is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu competitor that has won in the Pan American and World Championships. DeBlass also captured two titles in Ring Of Combat and got called to fight in the UFC. He is one of the only people in history to win the North American trials 3 times.
