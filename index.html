About Our Academy

Our Community

La Crosse American Jiu Jitsu offers a fun, clean, and safe environment for both adults and kids to learn and train in jiu-jitsu. We pride ourselves on having some of the friendliest members in the community.

Stress Relief

We encourage you to stop by and greet your new training partners. Discover firsthand how Jiu Jitsu can help you handle stress with ease, improve your breathing, and enhance your sleep quality.

Personal Growth

Forge lifelong friendships on the mat as you progress from a white to a black belt. The journey is as much about personal growth as it is about martial arts proficiency.

Inclusive Classes

No matter your age or lifestyle, La Crosse American Jiu Jitsu has a class for you. We offer specialized classes for Adults, Women, and Children, along with an open gym time on Saturday.

Looking for Women's Self Defense?

Join our empowering Women's Self-Defense Class and take the first step towards personal safety and confidence! In today's world, it's vital to be prepared for any situation, and our class is designed specifically with women in mind. Our program focuses on practical, easy-to-learn techniques that are effective in real-world scenarios.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to enhance your existing skills, our classes provide a supportive and encouraging environment for women of all ages and fitness levels. You'll learn how to recognize potential threats, assert boundaries, and defend yourself against physical attacks.
